Your Top 5 Questions, Answered

1. What's the story behind Branch?

Branch is an office furniture startup based in New York City. Founded in 2018, we’ve helped hundreds of teams in the United States and Canada furnish offices.

2. Where does Branch operate?

We deliver and install our office furniture across the continental United States and Ontario, Canada.

3. When should I start furnishing my office?

We recommend creating a furniture plan at least a month before you’re moving into a new space. But if you’re in a hurry, Branch can help you furnish in as little as a week.

4. What services does Branch include?

When you work with Branch, we handle it all, from figuring out your furniture layout to negotiating with your landlord about furniture delivery and insurance. For orders above $1,000, delivery and installation always free.

5. What's your return policy?

So long as it’s undamaged, return your furniture any time within 30 days of your delivery.
